Total Quality Management

Compliance & Pharmaceutical Supply Quality

EndoCare adopts a written and strict quality policy governing all stages of the supply chain. We believe that the reliability of hormones, vitamins, and pharmaceutical products begins with selecting licensed sources and ends with secure, digitally monitored storage to ensure patient safety and trustworthy healthcare.

GMP & GDP Regulatory Compliance Statement

We are absolutely committed to prohibiting the import or distribution of any product not manufactured in facilities compliant with internationally approved Good Manufacturing Practices (GMP). We also apply Good Distribution and Storage Practices (GDP) protocols across all our sub-warehouses and refrigerated transport networks to ensure 100% supply chain safety.

Specialized Logistics

Integrated Cold Chain

Digitally monitored cold storage and transport for heat-sensitive pharmaceutical and hormonal compounds.

1

Secure Import Storage

Receiving medical shipments at entry points in certified cooling containers and transporting them directly to our central warehouses equipped with the latest cooling systems (+2°C to +8°C, or +15°C to +25°C depending on the drug type).

2

Continuous Digital Monitoring

Using digital temperature recorders (Data Loggers) to monitor temperature and humidity inside warehouses and shipping containers around the clock, with instant alerts activated upon any deviation from approved temperature ranges.

3

Guaranteed Refrigerated Shipping

Delivering shipments to hospitals, agents, and pharmacies in all governorates using a fleet of equipped refrigerated trucks and insulated thermal shipping bags to ensure uninterrupted cold chain until receipt.

Supply & Consumer Safety

Track & Trace / Recall System

EndoCare adopts a precise digital tracking system that documents and records the movement of each pharmaceutical batch (Batch/Lot Number) from the manufacturing source to warehouses and then to the end customer. This system enables us to efficiently manage risks and ensure the safety and quality of all marketed medicines.

Instant Origin Verification

Linking the batch QR code to the Certificate of Analysis (CoA) and supporting documents to ensure product authenticity.

Emergency Recall Within 2 Hours

An approved emergency plan ensuring the ability to locate all packages of any batch and fully withdraw them from the market and customers within just 2 hours in case of international safety notifications.
